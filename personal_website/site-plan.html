Home page

[Hello, My Name is Ryker Davison and I am an Afghanistan veteran that is now going to school. I have always had an idea in my head about creating a t-shirt business. I know that there is already a bunch of Veteran owned and ran businesses that sells clothing. But I was thinking maybe I could bridge the gap between vet and civilians and just provide a fun and funny experience for everyone to enjoy. Lately it seems no matter what you do, you are somehow going to offend somebody. So to combat that I want to provide people with something they can wear unapologetically that will be funny and possibly open peoples eyes about how stupid it is to be offended by everything.]
